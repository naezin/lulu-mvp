## Description

The first app after NICU discharge. LULU.

Built specifically for parents of preterm babies, twins/triplets, and SGA (Small for Gestational Age) infants.

--- Key Features ---

[AI Cry Analysis - 100% On-Device]
Your baby's cry audio is NEVER sent to any server.
No recordings are stored on your device.
All analysis happens entirely on-device.

[Corrected Age Calculation]
Enter gestational weeks and each baby's corrected age is calculated automatically.
Twins can have different gestational ages. Each is calculated individually.

[Twins/Triplets Individual Management]
One-tap tab switching with BabyTabBar. Each baby gets independent records, timelines, and insights.

[5-Type Quick Recording]
Feeding (breast/bottle/mixed), Sleep, Diaper, Play, and Health records in under 3 seconds.
Designed for one-handed use at 3am by exhausted parents.

[Sweet Spot Sleep Prediction]
Learns your baby's patterns to predict the next optimal feeding and sleep time.

[Family Sharing]
Share records with your partner and grandparents using an invite code.

[Fenton/WHO Growth Charts]
Automatically switches between Fenton and WHO charts based on corrected age.
Track weight, height, and head circumference in percentiles.

--- Who is LULU for? ---

- Parents of preterm babies (born before 37 weeks)
- Parents of twins, triplets, or higher-order multiples
- Parents of SGA (Small for Gestational Age) babies
- Any parent who needs fast, accurate baby tracking

--- Medical Disclaimer ---

LULU is an information tool, not a medical device.
All health information is for reference only. Always consult your pediatrician for medical decisions.
